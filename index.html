About Our Company

Founded in 2014 in Johannesburg, South Africa, Nkokheli Creatives is a catalyst creative agency that continues to constructively push the boundaries and create conducive platforms that aid in the amelioration of youth unemployment in South Africa.

We are a comprehensive multi-media solutions company that works closely with our clients from the initial analysis and strategy formulation to the final implementation.
